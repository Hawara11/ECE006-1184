Topics related to artificial intelligience

Goals of Artificial Intelligience:

  1. Build Expert Systems − Systems that show smart behaviour, understand, illustrate, explain, and inform its users.

  2. Applying human intelligence in machines − Creating systems that understand, think, learn, and behave like humans.

What Contributes to AI?

Artificial Intelligence is a science and technology based on such disciplines as computer science , biology, psychology , linguistics, math, engineering, etc. A major thrust of AI is the development of human intelligence related machine functions such as reasoning, understanding, and problem-solving.
Out of the following areas, one or multiple areas can contribute to build an intelligent system.

What is Artificial Intelligience Technique?

In the real world, the knowledge has some unwelcomed properties:

  • It keeps changing constantly.

  • Its volume is huge, next to unimaginable.

  • It is not well-organized or well-formatted.

AI Technique is a manner to organize and use the knowledge efficiently in such a way that:

  • It should be useful in many situations though it is incomplete or inaccurate..

  • It should be perceivable by the people who provide it.

  • It should be easily modifiable to correct errors.

AI techniques elevate the speed of execution of the complex program it is equipped with.
